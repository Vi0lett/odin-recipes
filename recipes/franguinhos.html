Franguinhos

Description

This is probably my most used recipe. It's simple, healthy AND tasty.

Yes, it is a very simple grilled chicken recipe. But there is nothing wrong with simplicity!

Ingredients

  • Chicken fillet, also known as sasami
  • Olive oil
  • Salt to taste

    You probably need to add more than you expect, if you're a beginner.

  • Seasoning to taste

    Personally, I'm a fan of chimichurri
